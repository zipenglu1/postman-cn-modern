# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be useful, calm, and welcoming for people who are trying to improve Postman Desktop localization.

Participants are expected to:

- be respectful and constructive;
- assume good intent when possible;
- keep discussions focused on the project;
- avoid harassment, personal attacks, and discriminatory language;
- avoid posting private data, secrets, account information, or workspace content.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments that are abusive, off-topic, spam, or unsafe. Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent bans from the project.

If you need to report a problem, open an issue with minimal detail and ask for a private maintainer contact path.
